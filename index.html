Facts about Lions

Lions are king of jungle, they have massive strength and a roar which can shiver spines of other animals when they hear, lions live in groups.

Facts about Tiger

Tiger are very strong animal which has muscle mass higher even than lions they hunt alone without anyone's help , Tigers are fearless.

Facts about Elephant

Elephant are very calm animals and they are also very cute and their bond with humans are great and loving , elephants are huge in size.
